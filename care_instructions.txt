========================================
CARING FOR YOUR LÉIA
========================================

Every LÉIA piece is crafted to last generations, but proper care ensures it remains as beautiful as the day you first wore it. This guide provides detailed instructions for maintaining your jewelry and timepieces.

========================================
GENERAL JEWELRY CARE PRINCIPLES
========================================

THE GOLDEN RULES

1. LAST ON, FIRST OFF
Put your jewelry on after applying makeup, perfume, hairspray, and lotion. Remove it before bathing, swimming, or sleeping.

2. SEPARATE STORAGE
Store each piece separately to prevent scratching. Use individual pouches or compartments.

3. AVOID CHEMICALS
Remove jewelry before using cleaning products, chlorine pools, hot tubs, or handling harsh chemicals.

4. REGULAR INSPECTION
Check clasps, prongs, and settings regularly. If anything feels loose, visit a LÉIA boutique immediately.

5. PROFESSIONAL CLEANING
Take advantage of our complimentary cleaning service twice per year.

========================================
CARE BY METAL TYPE
========================================

18K GOLD (Yellow, White, Rose)

Daily Care:
- Wipe with soft jewelry cloth after wearing
- Avoid abrasive materials that can scratch
- Remove before heavy physical activity

Cleaning at Home:
- Warm water with mild soap
- Soft brush (like a baby toothbrush) for crevices
- Rinse thoroughly and dry completely
- Polish with dedicated gold polishing cloth

What to Avoid:
- Chlorine (pool, hot tub) - can weaken gold
- Harsh chemicals and abrasives
- Ultrasonic cleaners without professional supervision

Professional Service:
- Annual inspection of prongs and settings
- Professional polishing every 1-2 years to restore shine
- Rhodium re-plating for white gold if desired (every 2-3 years)

---

PLATINUM

Daily Care:
- Extremely durable but will develop natural patina over time
- Some customers love this "lived-in" look; others prefer high polish
- Wipe after wearing to prevent buildup

Cleaning at Home:
- Same as gold - warm soapy water and soft brush
- Platinum-specific polishing cloth available

Natural Patina:
- Platinum develops a subtle matte finish over time
- This is normal and valued by many collectors
- Can be professionally polished to restore mirror finish anytime

Professional Service:
- Annual inspection
- Professional polish if desired (every 1-2 years)
- Platinum is very secure for stone settings

---

STERLING SILVER

Daily Care:
- Silver will naturally tarnish - this is normal
- Store in anti-tarnish pouches when not wearing
- Wear often - body oils actually prevent tarnishing

Cleaning at Home:
- Warm soapy water and soft cloth
- For tarnish: silver polishing cloth or professional silver cleaner
- Avoid over-polishing textured or oxidized areas

Oxidized/Blackened Silver (Intentional Finish):
- DO NOT polish the darkened areas - this is intentional design
- Clean only the polished portions
- The contrast between dark and bright is part of the aesthetic
- Oxidation may naturally lighten over time - this is expected

Professional Service:
- Can re-apply oxidized finish if desired
- Annual inspection and cleaning

---

TITANIUM

Daily Care:
- Extremely durable and hypoallergenic
- Resistant to scratching and corrosion
- Very low maintenance

Cleaning at Home:
- Warm soapy water and soft cloth
- No special products needed
- Titanium doesn't tarnish or corrode

Special Coatings (PVD, DLC, Colored):
- These coatings are permanent and durable
- Avoid abrasive cleaners
- May show wear patterns over time - this is part of the piece's story

Professional Service:
- Minimal maintenance required
- Annual inspection of mechanisms and settings

---

BLACKENED/COATED METALS (Black Rhodium, PVD, DLC)

Daily Care:
- These coatings are durable but can wear over time
- Avoid abrasive contact
- High-wear areas (ring undersides) may show more wear

Cleaning:
- Soft cloth and warm water only
- NO polishing cloths or abrasive cleaners
- Professional cleaning recommended

Wear Patterns:
- Some wear is natural and expected
- Many customers appreciate the "earned" look
- Can be professionally re-coated if desired ($300-600)

Professional Service:
- Gentle cleaning only
- Re-coating available every 3-5 years if desired

========================================
CARE BY GEMSTONE TYPE
========================================

DIAMONDS

Durability: Extremely hard (10 on Mohs scale) but can chip if struck hard

Daily Care:
- Diamonds attract oils and can appear cloudy
- Wipe after wearing, especially rings
- Check prongs regularly

Cleaning:
- Warm soapy water and soft brush works perfectly
- Professional ultrasonic cleaning safe for most settings
- Avoid harsh impacts despite hardness

Settings to Watch:
- Pavé settings: Check for loose stones regularly
- Invisible settings: Professional inspection annually
- Prong settings: Have tightened if any movement detected

---

COLORED GEMSTONES (Sapphires, Emeralds, Rubies)

Sapphires (Blue, Yellow, Pink, Orange):
- Hardness: 9 (very durable)
- Can be cleaned like diamonds
- Avoid extreme temperature changes
- Safe for daily wear

Emeralds:
- More delicate than other stones
- Often treated with oils - avoid ultrasonic cleaning
- Clean with soft damp cloth only
- Avoid temperature changes and harsh impacts
- Professional cleaning recommended

Rubies:
- Hardness: 9 (very durable)
- Can be cleaned like diamonds
- Safe for daily wear
- Avoid harsh impacts

Garnets, Citrines, Amethysts:
- Moderate hardness (6.5-7.5)
- Clean with warm soapy water
- Avoid ultrasonic cleaners
- Can scratch more easily - store carefully

Turquoise:
- Porous stone - avoid water, chemicals, perfumes
- Clean with soft dry cloth only
- Can change color over time (this is natural)
- Keep away from heat and sunlight

---

PEARLS (Including Baroque Pearls)

Special Care Required:

Pearls are organic and delicate:
- Wipe with soft damp cloth after wearing (body oils and acids can damage)
- NEVER use jewelry cleaner, ultrasonic, or harsh chemicals
- Apply perfume and hairspray BEFORE putting on pearls
- Store separately from other jewelry (pearls are soft and scratch easily)

Cleaning:
- Soft damp cloth only
- Can use very mild soap if needed, rinse immediately
- Dry thoroughly before storing

Long-term Care:
- Pearls need moisture - wearing them helps
- If not worn regularly, store with slightly damp cloth
- Have restrung every 2-3 years if on silk cord
- Professional cleaning recommended annually

Natural Changes:
- Pearls may develop slight luster changes over time
- This is natural and part of their organic nature
- Professional assessment if concerned

---

MOONSTONE

Moderate Delicacy:
- Hardness: 6-6.5 (softer than most gems)
- Can scratch and chip - handle carefully
- Avoid harsh impacts and abrasives

Cleaning:
- Warm water and soft cloth only
- NO ultrasonic cleaners
- NO harsh chemicals or steam

Special Notes:
- Moonstone can crack if exposed to sudden temperature changes
- Beautiful internal "schiller" effect is natural
- Inclusions and natural imperfections are expected

---

AMBER

Organic Material - Special Care:

Very Soft and Delicate:
- Avoid all chemicals, perfumes, hairspray
- Clean with slightly damp soft cloth only
- Store separately (scratches very easily)
- Avoid heat and direct sunlight
- Can become brittle over time

Natural Changes:
- Color may deepen with age
- May develop fine surface cracks (this is natural)
- Some customers love the aged patina

Professional Care:
- Can be professionally polished to restore surface
- Keep away from ultrasonic cleaners

========================================
CARE BY PRODUCT TYPE
========================================

RINGS

High-Contact Area:
- Rings experience the most wear
- Remove before hand-intensive activities
- Check prongs monthly for looseness
- Consider rotating daily rings to extend life

Specific Care:
- Avoid wearing while lifting weights or exercising
- Remove before applying hand lotion (builds up under stones)
- Have professionally cleaned quarterly if worn daily

Sizing:
- Fingers swell in heat, shrink in cold
- If ring feels loose or tight, visit boutique for assessment
- Frequent resizing can weaken the metal over time

---

NECKLACES & PENDANTS

Chain Care:
- Chains can kink or break if pulled
- Store flat or hanging to prevent tangling
- Check clasps regularly for wear

Pendant Care:
- Ensure bail (loop) is secure
- Wipe pendant after wearing (picks up skin oils and makeup)
- Store pendant separately from chain if possible

Back Necklaces (Chrysalis, Wingspan):
- These pieces have multiple attachment points
- Professional fitting recommended initially
- Check all connection points before wearing
- Store flat with all parts attached to prevent losing pieces

---

BRACELETS

Daily Wear:
- Remove before sleeping (can catch on bedding)
- Avoid wearing during sports or heavy activity
- Check clasps regularly

Link Bracelets:
- Can be sized by adding/removing links
- Keep extra links in safe place
- Have links checked for security annually

Cuff Bracelets:
- Can be gently adjusted for fit
- Don't over-flex (metal fatigue)
- Store in round form (don't flatten)

---

EARRINGS

Post Earrings:
- Clean posts regularly (buildup from ears)
- Check backs for tightness
- If ears are sensitive, clean posts with rubbing alcohol before wearing

Ear Climbers & Cuffs:
- Adjust gently to fit your ear
- Don't over-bend
- Remove before sleeping
- Clean contact points regularly

Kinetic Earrings (Satellite Orbit):
- Mechanism is sealed - no home maintenance
- Professional check every 2 years
- Avoid dropping (precision mechanism)

Hoops:
- Check closure mechanism regularly
- Clean hinge points
- If closure feels loose, visit boutique

---

HAIR JEWELRY (Pins, Vines, Combs, Crowns)

Special Considerations:
- Remove before sleeping
- Avoid hairspray directly on jewelry
- Clean after each use (hair products build up)
- Store in protective case

Flexible Pieces (Spine Curve, Hair Vine):
- Don't over-bend
- Pins should be straight and secure
- Check for bent elements regularly

Combs & Crowns:
- Teeth/pins should be straight and secure
- Avoid forcing into tight hairstyles
- Can be adjusted professionally if needed

---

BROOCHES

Wearing:
- Pin mechanism should be tight and secure
- Don't pin through delicate fabrics (can damage both)
- Consider adding safety chain for heavy pieces

Storage:
- Store with pin closed
- Keep in individual compartment
- Check pin and clasp mechanism regularly

Enamel Brooches (Shield Insignia):
- Enamel can chip if dropped - handle carefully
- Don't clean enamel with chemicals
- Professional enamel repair available if chipped

========================================
WATCH CARE (VANTA COLLECTION)
========================================

AUTOMATIC WATCHES

Daily Wear:
- Automatic watches need motion to stay wound
- If not worn daily, may need manual winding
- Don't set date between 9 PM - 3 AM (can damage mechanism)

Water Resistance:
- Screw-down crowns MUST be fully tightened before water contact
- Have gaskets tested every 2 years
- After battery change or service, test water resistance before swimming

Cleaning:
- Wipe case and crystal with soft cloth
- For water-resistant watches: slightly damp cloth is fine
- Clean strap/bracelet separately

Crown Care:
- Don't force crown
- Screw-down crowns: make sure fully closed
- If crown feels stuck, bring to professional

Professional Service:
- Movement service every 4-5 years
- Includes cleaning, lubrication, regulation
- Earlier service if watch loses accuracy

Magnetic Fields:
- Keep away from strong magnets (speakers, MRI machines, magnetic phone mounts)
- If exposed, can be demagnetized professionally

---

DIGITAL WATCHES (Singularity)

E-ink Display:
- Very durable but avoid hard impacts
- Screen may temporarily fade in extreme cold (returns to normal)
- Clean with soft dry cloth

Charging:
- Charge wirelessly as needed
- Battery lasts approximately 6 months per charge
- Can be left on charger indefinitely

Software:
- Updates available via LÉIA app
- Check for updates quarterly

Water Resistance:
- Rated to 50m but charging port should be dry
- Rinse with fresh water after ocean/pool use

---

SKELETON WATCHES (Eclipse Skeleton)

Special Visibility Means Special Care:
- Dust visible through sapphire - more frequent professional cleaning
- Avoid exposing to dusty environments
- Don't attempt to clean movement yourself

Exhibition Casebacks (All Vanta Watches):
- Both sides are sapphire crystal
- Clean carefully to avoid scratching
- Beautiful to display on watch winder

========================================
SEASONAL CARE TIPS
========================================

SUMMER
- Remove jewelry before swimming (chlorine, salt water)
- Sunscreen and insect repellent can damage some stones
- Sweat can tarnish silver faster
- Store in cool, dry place

WINTER
- Fingers shrink in cold - rings may feel loose
- Remove jewelry before winter sports
- Salt on roads can damage some metals
- Dry thoroughly after exposure to snow/moisture

========================================
TRAVEL WITH LÉIA
========================================

Packing:
- Use individual pouches for each piece
- Consider a jewelry travel case with compartments
- Keep valuable pieces in carry-on luggage
- Take photos of pieces for insurance/records

During Travel:
- Remove jewelry before beach/pool
- Store securely in hotel safe
- Keep cleaning cloth with you for quick wipes

========================================
WHEN TO SEEK PROFESSIONAL HELP
========================================

Visit a LÉIA Boutique Immediately If:

- Stone feels loose or moves
- Clasp won't close properly
- Prong is bent or broken
- Chain is kinked
- Watch loses significant time
- Any damage from impact or accident
- Piece doesn't sit properly when worn

Don't attempt home repairs - some fixes require professional expertise and tools.

========================================
STORAGE BEST PRACTICES
========================================

Individual Storage:
- Store each piece separately to prevent scratching
- Use soft pouches or compartments
- Keep in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight

Anti-Tarnish Storage:
- Silver pieces benefit from anti-tarnish pouches
- Silica gel packets help control moisture
- Climate-controlled environments are ideal

Watch Storage:
- Automatic watches: watch winder if not worn regularly
- All watches: store away from magnets
- Original box is ideal for long-term storage

What NOT To Do:
- Don't store all jewelry together in one box
- Don't store in bathroom (humidity damages)
- Don't keep in direct sunlight (some stones can fade)
- Don't store pearls in completely sealed container (need some air)

========================================
LÉIA CARE PHILOSOPHY
========================================

We believe in jewelry that lives with you. Some wear, patina, and evolution is natural and beautiful - it tells the story of your journey with the piece.

However, proper care ensures your LÉIA jewelry remains structurally sound and continues to bring you joy for decades.

When in doubt, visit any LÉIA boutique. Our advisors are trained to assess and advise on the best care for your specific piece.

Remember: The best care for jewelry is to wear it. Pieces that are worn regularly often fare better than those hidden away.
