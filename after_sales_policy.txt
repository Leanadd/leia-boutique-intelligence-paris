========================================
COMMITMENT TO EXCELLENCE
========================================

At LÉIA, our relationship with you doesn't end at purchase. Every piece of LÉIA jewelry and every Vanta timepiece is backed by our commitment to lifetime service and support.

This document outlines our after-sales services, warranty coverage, repair procedures, and return policies.

========================================
WARRANTY COVERAGE
========================================

HIGH JEWELRY WARRANTY (Eclipse, Hatching, Amazon Collections)

Coverage Period: 2 years from date of purchase

What's Covered:
- Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship
- Stone loss due to setting failure
- Clasp or closure mechanism failure
- Metal discoloration or tarnishing (excluding intentional finishes like oxidized silver or blackened details)
- Structural integrity issues

What's NOT Covered:
- Normal wear and tear (scratches, minor dents)
- Damage from accidents, misuse, or improper care
- Loss or theft
- Damage from unauthorized repairs
- Natural patina development on metals (this is expected and part of the piece's evolution)
- Pearls and organic gemstones (these naturally change over time)

---

VANTA WATCHES WARRANTY

Coverage Period: 5 years from date of purchase (movements only), 2 years (case, crystal, strap)

What's Covered:
- Swiss movement defects or malfunctions
- Water resistance failure (if properly maintained)
- Crown, pushers, or bezel mechanism failure
- Crystal scratching (sapphire crystal only, one-time replacement)
- Exhibition caseback seal failure

What's NOT Covered:
- Battery replacement (digital watches)
- Strap wear or replacement
- Cosmetic damage to case or bezel
- Water damage if crown was not properly secured
- Damage from magnetic fields, extreme temperatures, or shock
- Loss of water resistance after case opening by unauthorized service

REPAIR SERVICES
========================================

COMPLIMENTARY SERVICES (Lifetime)

Available to all LÉIA owners, regardless of warranty status:
- Professional cleaning and polishing (twice per year)
- Clasp and closure adjustment or tightening
- Prong inspection and tightening
- Chain and link inspection
- Basic resizing (rings and bracelets, within 2 sizes)
- Strap adjustment for watches

PAID REPAIR SERVICES

Stone Replacement:

- Colored gemstones: Cost of stone + $200 setting fee

Metal Repairs:
- Minor soldering or reattachment: $200-400
- Significant reconstruction: Quote provided after assessment
- Re-plating or re-coating (black rhodium, PVD, etc.): $300-600

Watch Services:
- Movement servicing: $400-800 depending on complexity
- Water resistance testing and seal replacement: $150
- Crystal replacement: $200-500 depending on size
- Full restoration: Quote provided after assessment

Complex Repairs:
- Kinetic mechanism servicing (Satellite Orbit Earrings, etc.): $500+
- Invisible setting repair: $400+
- Enamel restoration: $600+
- Custom modifications: Quote provided

========================================
REPAIR PROCESS
========================================

Step 1: ASSESSMENT
Bring your piece to any LÉIA boutique or contact our customer service. We provide free assessment within 48 hours.

Step 2: QUOTE
For paid repairs, we provide a detailed quote including timeline (typically 4-8 weeks for complex work). No work begins without your approval.

Step 3: EXPERT REPAIR
All repairs are performed by LÉIA-trained artisans or, for watches, certified Swiss watchmakers. We maintain the highest standards.

Step 4: QUALITY CONTROL
Every repaired piece undergoes rigorous inspection before return.

Step 5: RETURN & GUARANTEE
All repairs come with a 6-month guarantee on the work performed.

RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY
========================================

30-DAY RETURN WINDOW

You may return or exchange any unworn LÉIA piece within 30 days of purchase for:
- Full refund (original payment method)
- Exchange for another piece
- Store credit

Conditions:
- Item must be unworn, in original condition
- Original packaging and certificate of authenticity must be included
- Engraved pieces cannot be returned (personalization is permanent)
- Special order or custom pieces cannot be returned

---

SIZING EXCHANGES (Rings & Bracelets)

If your ring or bracelet doesn't fit:
- First resize is complimentary within 60 days of purchase (within 2 sizes)
- Exchange for different size is complimentary within 30 days
- After 60 days, standard resizing fees apply

---

CHANGE OF MIND

We understand that jewelry is personal. If a piece doesn't feel right:
- Return within 30 days for full refund, no questions asked
- We want you to love what you wear

VANTA WATCHES

Regular Servicing Schedule:
- Automatic watches: Every 4-5 years for movement service

Between Services:

If you lose a stone from your LÉIA piece:

2. We source an identical replacement (or closest possible match)
3. Our master setters restore the piece to original condition
4. Cost: Stone price + $150-200 setting fee

Priority service available: 5-business-day turnaround for additional fee
