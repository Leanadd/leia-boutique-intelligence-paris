========================================
BRAND IDENTITY
========================================

LÉIA is a Parisian high jewelry and Swiss watchmaking house founded in 2013, the year marriage equality became law in France. Born from a vision of jewelry as liberation rather than ornament, LÉIA creates pieces that celebrate all forms of identity and emancipation.

The name LÉIA subtly integrates "IA" (intelligence artificielle), reflecting our belief that the future of luxury lies in the intersection of ancestral craftsmanship and contemporary innovation.

Our tagline - Unbound - is both promise and philosophy. We create jewelry unbound by gender conventions, unbound by traditional definitions of femininity and masculinity, unbound by the limitations others might place on who can wear what.

========================================
FOUNDING STORY
========================================

LÉIA was founded in Paris in 2013, a pivotal year when France legalized marriage for all. This moment of societal transformation inspired our founders to create a jewelry house that would embody the same spirit of evolution and inclusivity.

While rooted in Parisian design heritage, LÉIA partners with Swiss master watchmakers for our timepiece collections, combining French artistic vision with Swiss mechanical precision.

From the beginning, LÉIA rejected the traditional binary approach to jewelry design. We don't create "men's collections" and "women's collections." We create collections that speak to human experiences: transformation, power, fluidity, innovation.

========================================
BRAND VALUES
========================================

EMANCIPATION THROUGH DESIGN
Every piece is designed to liberate, not constrain. Our jewelry celebrates personal evolution and the freedom to define oneself.

INCLUSIVITY AS FOUNDATION
LÉIA embraces all identities. Our Eclipse collection specifically honors trans, non-binary, and gender-fluid individuals, while our other collections celebrate different facets of feminine power and expression.

CRAFTSMANSHIP MEETS INNOVATION
We honor traditional high jewelry techniques - hand engraving, stone setting, lost-wax casting - while embracing cutting-edge materials like titanium, ceramic, and advanced coatings.

INTELLIGENCE IN EVERY DETAIL
From our name to our designs, intelligence guides our choices. Each piece is thoughtfully conceived to carry meaning beyond mere aesthetics.

========================================
OUR COLLECTIONS
========================================

ECLIPSE COLLECTION - Gender-Fluid
"Beyond definition"

The Eclipse collection represents identity in transition, the space between, the refusal of binary thinking. These pieces use innovative materials (titanium, blackened metals, kinetic mechanisms) and conceptual designs (Möbius topology, binary code, orbital movement) to celebrate fluidity.

Key pieces: Möbius Infinity Ring, Satellite Orbit Earrings, Gravity Defying Hoops, Binary Identity Ring

Materials: Titanium, platinum, mixed metals, moonstone, innovative mechanisms
Price range: $6,800 - $18,900
Philosophy: For those who exist beyond categories and definitions

---

HATCHING COLLECTION - Feminine Delicate Power
"Emergence and becoming"

Hatching celebrates the poetic strength of femininity - delicate but unbreakable, soft yet architectural. Inspired by the moment before emergence, these pieces capture transformation in progress. Light, movement, and organic forms define this collection.

Key pieces: Chrysalis Back Drape, Spine Curve Hair Pin, Echoes of Voice Drop Earrings, Warrior's Thread Choker

Materials: 18K gold (white, yellow, rose), diamonds, baroque pearls
Price range: $6,900 - $22,500
Philosophy: Power doesn't need to shout. Elegance as strength.

---

AMAZON COLLECTION - Powerful Feminine
"Unapologetic presence"

The Amazon collection is for those who command attention without apology. Bold, geometric, colorful - these pieces make statements. Large stones, substantial weight, and imposing designs celebrate feminine power in its most visible form.

Key pieces: Conquest Statement Cuff, Sovereign Collar Necklace, Warrior Queen Ring, Lioness Mane Hair Comb

Materials: 18K gold, colored gemstones (emeralds, rubies, sapphires, citrine, turquoise), enamel
Price range: $9,800 - $35,000
Philosophy: Wear your power visibly. Presence is earned, not given.

---

VANTA WATCHES COLLECTION - Tech Minimalist
"Time reimagined"

Named after Vantablack, the world's darkest material, this collection represents time measured by personal standards. These Swiss mechanical and innovative digital timepieces reject traditional watch aesthetics in favor of radical minimalism and technical innovation.

Key pieces: Void Automatic, Eclipse Skeleton, Singularity Digital, Quantum Minimal

Materials: Black ceramic, titanium, DLC coating, sapphire crystal, e-ink displays
Price range: $18,500 - $35,000
Philosophy: Time belongs to those who seize it. Function without decoration.

CRAFTSMANSHIP & MATERIALS
========================================

JEWELRY TECHNIQUES
- Hand engraving and texturing
- Invisible stone setting
- Lost-wax casting for organic forms

MATERIALS PALETTE
Traditional: 18K gold (yellow, white, rose), platinum, sterling silver

BOUTIQUE EXPERIENCE

Every LÉIA purchase includes:
